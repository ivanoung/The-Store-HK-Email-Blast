Take it easy, kale

Rhythm Superfoods Organic Kale Chips are a delicious, nutrient-dense alternative to standard fried chips.

Rhythm Superfoods’ Kale Chips are handcrafted in small batches by lightly tossing fresh organic kale in a dressing made with sunflower seeds, sesame seeds, vegetables, fruits, herbs and spices.

Rather than frying the chips, they are air-crisped under a low heat in order to maintain the living enzymes in the kale and maximize nutritional potency.
